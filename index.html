About

Caleb Thompson is a VFX Compositor in the commercial, film, and television industry. Some of his most notable credits include: The Hobbit, Red Tails, Red Riding Hood, Premium Rush, The Imaginarium of Doctor Parnassus and 30 minutes or less.
